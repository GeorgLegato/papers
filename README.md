# Papers

This repository collects compact working papers around MELT, a speculative
information-geometry framework for reading physical measurements as transitions
between underlying bookkeeping structures and their observable readouts.

The notes here are exploratory. They try to keep three layers separate:

- the published experimental or observational input,
- the standard operator or model language used by the field,
- the additional MELT interpretation proposed on top of that input.

## Papers

- [Paper_VectorThresholds](Paper_VectorThresholds/) — a compact MELT reading of
  LHCb arXiv:2603.12477, focusing on the vector-sector threshold at the
  open-charm `DD*` gate.
